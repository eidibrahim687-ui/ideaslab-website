About IDEAS Lab

IDEAS Lab is a research institute focused on evidence-based development, data analytics, and policy research. The institute conducts interdisciplinary research on development, climate change, population dynamics, and economic policy.

Our mission is to generate high-quality research and innovative solutions that support sustainable development and informed decision-making.
